About our cafe

Kopi Tiga Sore is a coffee shop spread throughout Indonesia. Kopi Tiga Sore is the largest coffee shop in Indonesia, with 350 stores in 34 countries, including 50 in the Jakarta, 47 in Jember, 36 in Malang, 18 in the Surabaya, 14 in Bandung, 29 in Tangerang, 24 in Jogyakarta, 18 in Padang, 39 in the Lombok, 19 in Palembang and 33 in Bali.

Kopi Tiga Sore sells hot and cold drinks, coffee beans, salads, hot and cold sandwiches, sweet pastries, snacks, and items such as glasses and tumblers.
